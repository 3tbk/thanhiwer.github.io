About

About Me

I am a research scholar in the Watershed Sustainability lab at the University of South Florida, USA. I am also a JSPS Research Fellow in the Global Hydrodynamics Lab at the University of Tokyo, Japan. Before that, I completed a postdoc study at the Resilient Water Systems Group at Singapore University of Technology and Design, Singapore and a PhD study at the Mekong Flows Group at the University of Canterbury, New Zealand.

My research interestests include modeling of surface and groundwater systems, water-energy-food nexus, reservoir operation, optimization, water quality, fluvial morphology, climate change, hydraulic structure design, remote sensing, machine learning, and spatial analysis.
