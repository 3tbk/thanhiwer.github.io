About

About Me

I am a postdoc research scholar in the Watershed Sustainability group and Modelling and Assessment of Sustainable Systems (MASS) group at the University of South Florida, USA. I have been a JSPS Fellow and now a collaborator in the Global Hydrodynamics lab at the University of Tokyo, Japan. Before that, I was a research fellow at the Resilient Water Systems group at Singapore University of Technology and Design, Singapore, and a PhD candidate in the Mekong Flows group at the University of Canterbury, New Zealand. I have served as an assistant editor for Vietnam Journal of Science, Technology and Engineering (Ministry of Science and Technology, Vietnam).

My research interestests include surface and subsurface hydrology, hydraulics, climate-water-energy nexus, water infrastructure management and operations, river processes, biogeochemical processes, climate change, and hydraulic structure design. My methodology includes numerical modeling, remote sensing, optimization, machine learning, geospatial analysis, and data analystics.
